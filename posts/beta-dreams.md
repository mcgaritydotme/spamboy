I've been testing the iOS 27 beta on my iPad Mini the past couple days, because I wanted to get a glimpse of what a smart Siri could do for me. So far, it's impressive — enough so that I'd consider upgrading my daily carry device were I not so broke!

I've been [sleeping poorly](../insomnia/) lately, I went to bed on-time.  Despite that measure, I tossed & turned with an intense dream, where I was spending time with my late sister Micha.  It was now, 12 years after she passed.  We were passing my iPad Mini back and forth, challenging Siri AI to confirm which of us was the smartest, the prettiest, etc.  You know, typical sibling shit.

Then I woke up.
