I fill notebooks constantly and almost never open the old ones. For years I thought this was a failure. I have changed my mind.

## The act, not the archive

The value was never in the rereading. It was in the *writing down* &mdash; the half-second of editing your own thought as it leaves your head and hits the page. That edit is the whole point.

The notebook is a sieve, not a vault.

## What survives

The ideas worth keeping have a way of resurfacing on their own. If I have to go digging for it, it probably wasn&rsquo;t load-bearing. The good stuff knocks on the door again.
